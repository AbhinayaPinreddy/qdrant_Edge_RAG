Master Services Agreement (Sample Legal Corpus)

1. Governing Law
This Agreement is made between the parties and shall be governed by the laws of India.
All legal interpretation and enforcement shall be subject to competent courts in Bengaluru, Karnataka, unless arbitration is triggered under this Agreement.

2. Confidentiality
The parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information disclosed under this Agreement.
Confidential information includes business plans, source code, customer data, security procedures, and pricing terms.
Any breach of confidentiality shall result in immediate termination rights, injunctive relief rights, and possible monetary damages.
Each party shall ensure employees and contractors sign confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than this clause.

3. Payment Terms
All invoices must be settled within 30 days from the invoice date.
Payments delayed beyond 30 days may attract interest at 1% per month or the maximum lawful rate, whichever is lower.
Failure to pay may result in suspension of services after a 7-day cure notice.
Disputed invoices must be reported in writing within 10 days of receipt, with specific reasons and supporting records.
Undisputed portions of any invoice remain payable on time.

4. Service Levels
The service provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain 99.5% monthly uptime, excluding scheduled maintenance.
Scheduled maintenance shall be communicated at least 48 hours in advance where reasonably possible.
If uptime falls below 99.5%, the customer may request service credits as specified in the service schedule.

5. Data Protection and Security
Each party shall implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures.
Personal data processing, if any, shall comply with applicable data protection laws and written processing instructions.
Security incidents affecting customer data must be reported without undue delay and, where feasible, within 72 hours of discovery.

6. Intellectual Property
All pre-existing intellectual property remains with the original owner.
No transfer of ownership occurs unless explicitly stated in a signed written amendment.
Unauthorized use, reproduction, or distribution of proprietary materials is strictly prohibited.
Customer content remains customer-owned, and provider receives only a limited license required to perform services.

7. Warranties and Disclaimers
Each party warrants it has authority to enter this Agreement.
Except as expressly stated, services are provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis.
No implied warranties of merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, or non-infringement are provided, to the extent permitted by law.

8. Limitation of Liability
Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, special, punitive, or consequential damages.
Aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim, except for confidentiality breaches, fraud, or willful misconduct.

9. Indemnity
Provider shall defend and indemnify customer against third-party claims alleging provider materials infringe intellectual property rights, subject to prompt notice and cooperation.
Customer shall indemnify provider for claims arising from customer data, unlawful instructions, or misuse of services.

10. Term and Termination
This Agreement starts on the effective date and continues until terminated.
Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach is not cured within 30 days after written notice.
Either party may terminate for insolvency, bankruptcy filing, or cessation of business operations by the other party.
Upon termination, each party shall return or delete confidential information as required by law and contract.

11. Dispute Resolution
All disputes shall first be escalated to executive representatives for good-faith negotiation.
If unresolved within 30 days, disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in Bengaluru under applicable arbitration law.
The arbitration proceedings shall be conducted in English.

12. Notices and Miscellaneous
Formal notices must be in writing and sent by recognized courier, registered post, or official email channels.
Failure to enforce a provision does not waive future enforcement rights.
If any provision is held unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full force and effect.
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between parties and supersedes prior discussions on the same subject matter.
